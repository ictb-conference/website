International Conference on Timber Bridges

2015      Rotorua, New Zealand

 

Overview

The International Conference on Timber Bridges is a triennial meeting point for engineers, architects, experts, as well as scientists and students from all over the world in the field of timber bridges, including highway, railway and pedestrian structures.

Location

Rotorua, New Zealand's geothermal heartland, offers world-class conference facilities, spectacular landscapes and city sophistication that will leave conference attendees with unforgettable experiences.

Wharariki Beach, located near the northern tip of the South Island of New Zealand, became a must-see tourist destination following its popularity as the default lock screen image in the Windows 10 operating system.
